What we stand for

Our values

Depth over breadth

We'd rather publish one excellent deep-dive per week than five shallow summaries per day. Quality is the only metric we optimize for.

Always practical

No fluff, no theory-only. Every article includes real code, real trade-offs, and things you can apply immediately in your work.

Honest opinions

We call out bad patterns even in popular tools. We don't write sponsored content. Our reputation depends on your trust.

Community first

We read every comment and reply to every DM. The best ideas for articles come from our readers, and that's by design.
